<!--Equipament-->

* 1 Scintrex ENVI-CS magnetometer;

* 1 Scintrex ENVI-MAG magnetometer;

* 1 Scintrex CG-5 digital gravimeter;

* 1 Terraplus RS-230 gamma-ray spectrometer;

* 1 Terraplus KT-10 magnetic susceptibility meter;

* 1 Trimble Pro-XT DGPS;

* 2 Sthill MS 250 drilling rigs and 14 1" drills for rock coring;

* 1 Brunton Compass;

* 1 Pomeroy sample orienting fixture OR-2;

* 1 triaxial cell for geomechanical measurements (max pressure 100 MPa), Martins Campelo Testing Systems LTDA

* 3 notebooks Dell Latitude 5501 XCTO, Intel® Core i7-9850H, RAM 16 Gb, HD 2.0 Tb;

* 1 workstation, Intel core i7 2,8 GHz, RAM 128 Gb, HD 2.0 Tb;

* 5 workstations Dell Precision 5820 Tower XCTO Base, Intel Xeon W-2145, RAM 128 Gb, HD 2.5 Tb;

* 2 computers Dell OptiPlex 7070 SFF BTX, Intel® Core™ i7-9700, RAM 8 Gb, HD 1.0 Tb;

* 1 computer Dell Vostro 3470, Core i5-9400, RAM 8 Gb, HD 1.0 Tb;

* 1 computer HP-610, Intel® Core™ i5, RAM 6 Gb, HD 1.5 Tb;
